VoiceBox Download Page GitHub

Speak with your mouth movements

Multimodal AI-powered Lip Reading tool for people with dysphonia or aphonia. Predicts your words, lets you correct them, then speaks them aloud. Runs fully on your device. No internet required.

It watches your lip movements and listens to your speech (no matter how limited it is), uses AI to reconstruct the words you're trying to say. We believe every word should be heard.

Install
1

Download VoiceBox-Setup-x64.exe above and run it.

2

If Windows SmartScreen appears, click More info → Run anyway. The app is not yet code-signed.

3

Launch VoiceBox from the Start menu. Allow camera access when prompted.

4

Hit Prepare camera & audio, wait for the green indicators, then start speaking.
