Speak with your mouth movements

AI lip-reading and audio processing for people with dysphonia or aphonia. Predicts your words, lets you correct them, then speaks them aloud. Runs fully on your device — no cloud, no subscription.

What you get
Visual lip-reading AI Optional microphone fusion Editable word-by-word output Text-to-speech playback Phrase history & learning Fully offline
Install
1

Download VoiceBox-Setup-x64.exe above and run it.

2

If Windows SmartScreen appears, click More info → Run anyway. The app is not yet code-signed.

3

Launch VoiceBox from the Start menu. Allow camera access when prompted.

4

Hit Prepare camera & audio, wait for the green indicators, then start speaking.
